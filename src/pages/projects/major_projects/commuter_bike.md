This year's bike build was a drop bar commuter bike for my dad. The geometry is based on the Neuhaus Epyon, with a few modifications for riding with panniers.

<p style="text-align: center;">I machined billet dropouts designed by Daniel Yang. I also made a custom flat mount brake mount with kickstand mounts on the 5 axis mill. </p>

<div class="markdown_img_container">
<img class="markdown_image" src="/major_projects/commuter_bike/chainstays.webp">

</div>


<p style="text-align: center;">This time I welded the rear triangle first to give me better access to the trickier welds. I started with the chainstays assembly, then welded the seat tube and seatstays.</p>
